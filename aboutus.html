About The Magazine

Defining the Art of
Ceramic & Living.

**Ceramic Bangladesh** is the premier bi-monthly authority on Architecture, Real Estate, and Construction. We are the voice of a $460 billion GDP nation's most vital sector.

An initiative of the BCMEA, we connect Influencers, policymakers, and industry leaders across the globe—from Dhaka to Tokyo, London to New York.
